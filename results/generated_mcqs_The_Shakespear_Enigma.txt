## MCQ 1
Question: What unusual characteristic did the fruits of the Shakespear tree possess?
A) They were shaped like apples.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue.
C) They tasted like grapes.
D) They grew only during the winter.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did the villagers believe about the Shakespear tree?
A) It brought bad luck.
B) It held magical properties.
C) It was home to fairies.
D) It bore poisonous fruit.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: What was Emma's dream?
