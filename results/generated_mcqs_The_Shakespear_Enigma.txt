## MCQ 1
Question: What was unusual about the pears on the Shakespear tree?
A) They were poisonous.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue.
C) They tasted like apples.
D) They grew upside down.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did the villagers believe about the Shakespear tree?
A) It brought bad luck.
B) It held magical properties.
C) It was cursed.
D) It attracted dangerous animals.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 3
Question: What happened after Emma ate a Shakespear pear?
A) She fell asleep.
B) She experienced a whirlwind of visions.
C) She became ill.
D) She forgot her dreams.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 4
Question: How did the villagers react to Emma's passion for writing?
A) They discouraged her.
B) They were indifferent.
C) They began sharing their own tales.
D) They left the village.
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 5
Question: What became of Eldermere after Emma published her book?
A) It became a ghost town.
B) It became a hub of storytelling.
C) It became famous for its pear orchards.
D) It became a popular tourist destination for its unique architecture.
Correct Answer: B
