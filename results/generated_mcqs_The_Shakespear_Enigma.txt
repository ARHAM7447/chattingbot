## MCQ 1
Question: What unusual characteristic did the fruits of the Shakespear tree possess?
A) They were shaped like apples.
B) They shimmered with a golden hue.
C) They tasted like strawberries.
D) They grew upside down.
Correct Answer: B

## MCQ 2
Question: What did Emma dream of becoming?
A) A painter
B) A singer
C) A writer
D) A dancer
Correct Answer: C

## MCQ 3
Question: What happened after Emma ate a Shakespear fruit?
A) She fell asleep.
B) She experienced a whirlwind of visions about her future.
C) She lost her memory.
D) She gained the ability to fly.
Correct Answer: B
